About Me

I'm a MIS and Cybersecurity student at Florida Atlantic University with a deep passion for building real infrastructure — not just studying it. From spinning up Proxmox clusters and TrueNAS arrays to deploying cloud honeypots that catch live threat actors, I learn by doing.

My homelab is my proving ground: every project here reflects a skill I deliberately built. I'm focused on systems, networking, and practical security, and I'm working toward CompTIA Network+ and Security+ certifications to formalize what I've already been doing hands-on.

When I'm not in the lab, I work as a Technology Support Technician at FAU, helping the university community stay connected and productive.
